Henghui Ding

Ph.D. Candidate

Biography [CV]

Henghui Ding is currently a final year Ph.D. student (2016-now) in Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, advised by Prof. Xudong Jiang and Dr. Gang Wang. Prior to that, he received the B.E. degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU), Xi'an, China, in 2016. His research interests include computer vision, machine learning, with their applications to semantic segmentation and pose estimation.
